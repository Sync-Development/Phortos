Consultants: your partner in FAIR

Professional consulting, training and services to help realise the Internet of FAIR Data & Services




Vision

It is our vision to help establish the global Internet of FAIR Data & Services (IFDS) as a common environment for data-driven life sciences research and innovation around the world.

Mission

Phortos Consultants contributes to the development of the Internet of FAIR Data & Services through professional strategic consulting, training and data FAIRification services.

Strategy


Phortos Consultant’s strategy for its continued support to develop elements of the Internet of FAir Data and Services is to provide FAIR data consulting, FAIR Data Stewardship courses and data FAIRification Services. We are supporting the FAIR Principles and adhere to the GO FAIR recommended Implementation choices. To facilitate coherence and avoid reinventing of wheels we are a member of several GO FAIR Implementation Networks.

Mission


Phortos Consultants offers a variety of services ranging from strategic consulting, end user training and services to actively making data FAIR, all providing the basis for effectively enabling analytics driven capabilities.

1. Consulting - Enabling organisations to leverage the Internet of FAIR Data & Services

2. FAIR Training - Training the next generation of enablers of the Internet of FAIR Data & Services

3. FAIR Services - Transforming data for the Internet of FAIR Data & Services
